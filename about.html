About the Project

A Minecraft Fan Film Adventure

The Eyes in the Shadow is a cinematic Minecraft fan story created by an international team of passionate creators. From Austria to Australia, we are a group of storytellers, animators, and gamers working together to bring a powerful, mysterious, and humorous adventure to life.

Blending epic battles, emotional arcs, and a healthy dose of chaotic humor (and one very clever pig), our project aims to entertain, inspire, and showcase the creative power of the Minecraft community. Everything is animated in Blender and powered by imagination.

This website is a tribute to that journey — minimal, immersive, and open-source — just like Minecraft itself.
